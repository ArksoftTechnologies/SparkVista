User Guide

Everything you need to know to use Spark Laundry.

1 Getting Started

To start using our services, you'll need to create an account.

  1. Click Get Started or Log In in the top menu.
  2. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password).
  3. Once registered, you'll be redirected to your personal dashboard.

2 Using the Dashboard

Your dashboard is the command center.

  • Overview: See recent orders and status.
  • New Order: Quickly book a pickup.
  • History: View past transactions.

3 Placing an Order

Ready to wash?

  1. Click New Order in the sidebar.
  2. Select items or choose "Bulk Wash".
  3. Pick a date and time for pickup.
  4. Confirm your address and submit!
